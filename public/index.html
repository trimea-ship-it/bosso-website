The Origin

Rooted in Java Ijen’s
volcanic highlands.

BOSSO Origin works through an exclusive farming network in Bondowoso, East Java — the heart of Indonesia’s “Republik Kopi” region. Java Ijen coffee has been prized since the VOC era (1818), shaped by ancient volcanic soil from Mount Ijen, Raung, and Argopuro, cool highland climate, and meticulous post-harvest preparation within 20 hours of picking.

Craft & Process

From volcanic slope

Selective Harvest

Post-Harvest Processing

Buyer FAQ

Answers for
green coffee buyers.

What are the green bean specifications?

Blue Mountain / Yellow Catura / Java Typica Mix from 1,200–1,300+ masl. Full Washed, Natural, or Wine process. Moisture 10–12%, Screen 15–18, Grade 1 (max 8 defects/300g), cupping score 83+ SCA. Robusta Organic (Argopuro, 700–900 masl) also available seasonally.

How do buyers place an order?

Send destination country, volume, buyer type, and preferred Incoterms via WhatsApp or email. Lead time is 14–21 days from confirmed PO.

Are samples available?

Yes. Minimum sample is 1kg for evaluation before commercial order confirmation.

Which certifications are available?

Organic certificate available. Phytosanitary ready. Full export document set: ICO Certificate of Origin, Phytosanitary, Organic, COA, and Bill of Lading.

What shipping terms are offered?

FOB Surabaya standard. CIF available by request depending on destination.

What is the MOQ and export capacity?

Commercial MOQ is 1 ton in 60kg GrainPro-lined jute sacks. Export capacity up to 5 containers per month.
